Grilled Cheese Sandwich

A comfort food classic. This is a simple recipe in which only three ingredients are required: Toast, sliced cheese, and butter. The melted cheese provides a gooey satisfaction in your mouth.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 slices of toast
  • 2 slices of sliced cheese (American, Cheddar, Swiss etc.)

    • Steps

    1. In a frying pan, melt butter on medium heat
    2. Place 2 slices of cheese in between 2 slices of toast. Place in hot frying pan and let toast brown on one side before flipping to another.
    3. When toast is browned and toasted on both sides, remove from frying pan, if cheese is melted, slice sandwich in half if not, continue to warm until cheese melts.
    4. Serve, eat, and enjoy with drink of choice.
