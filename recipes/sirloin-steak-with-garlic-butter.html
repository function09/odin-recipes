Sirloin Steak with Garlic Butter

This is a very delicious and easy to make steak recipe. Garlic butter is added to this steak to further add richness and flavor. To achieve a great sear, be sure to let the steak reach room temperature before searing.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 4 pounds beef top sirloin steaks
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Melt butter in a small spacepan over medium-low heat; stir in minced garlic and garlic powder. Set aside.
  3. Season both sides of each steak with salt and pepper.
  4. Place steaks on preheated grill and cook 4 to 5 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 60 degrees celsius for medium doneness
  5. Transfer steaks to plates, brush with garlic butter and allow to rest for 5 minutes before consuming.
